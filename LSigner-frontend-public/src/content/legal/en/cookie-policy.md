# Cookie Policy

**Last updated:** Saturday, July 4, 2026

## 1. What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files that a website stores in the User's browser to remember information about their visit, such as their preferences or session status.

## 2. What cookies does LSigner use?

LSigner uses **exclusively its own technical and functional cookies**, necessary for the proper functioning of the Platform. LSigner **does not use analytical cookies or third-party cookies for advertising or tracking purposes**.

| Cookie       | Purpose                                                                                            | Type                     | Duration                                                                    |
| ------------ | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | ------------------------ | --------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| `session_id` | Keep the User's session active after login, including the multi-factor authentication (MFA) status | Technical / necessary    | Session (deleted when the browser is closed or after inactivity expiration) |
| `theme`      | Remember the visual theme preference (light/dark) chosen by the User                               | Functional / preferences | Persistent (up to 12 months or until the User modifies it)                  |
| `locale`     | Remember the User's preferred navigation language                                                  | Functional / preferences | Persistent (up to 12 months or until the User modifies it)                  |

## 3. Legal basis and consent

According to Article 22 of the LSSI-CE, cookies strictly necessary for the provision of the service requested by the User themselves (such as `session_id`) **do not require prior consent**, as without them the Platform could not function properly (for example, it would not be possible to keep the session active after logging in).

Preference cookies (`theme` and `lang`) are also considered functional in nature and have a low impact on User privacy, as they are limited to storing a display preference without any type of tracking or profiling.

LSigner does not install analytical or advertising cookies, so it is not necessary to display a granular cookie consent configuration panel beyond an informational notice.

## 4. Proprietary analytics system (clarification)

LSigner has an internal and self-hosted analytics system to understand the aggregated use of the Platform (time spent, most visited sections, etc.). This system **does not use cookies or any type of storage in the User's browser**: it processes the IP address of each request temporarily, exclusively for the calculation of aggregated statistics, and does not retain this information in an individualized manner. For this reason, this system is not subject to this Cookie Policy, although it is also mentioned in the [Privacy Policy](/legal/privacy-policy) as it involves the processing of personal data (the IP address).

## 5. How to manage or delete cookies

The User can allow, block, or delete the cookies installed in their browser at any time thru their browser settings. Below are some help links for the most common browsers:

- Google Chrome: chrome://settings/cookies
- Mozilla Firefox: browser privacy and security settings
- Safari: browser privacy preferences
- Microsoft Edge: cookie settings and site permissions

The User should be aware that if they block or delete the necessary technical cookies (`session_id`), they may not be able to log in or use the Platform's functionalities correctly.

## 6. Changes to this Policy

LSigner may update this Cookie Policy in case of incorporating new cookies or modifying the purposes of the existing ones. Any relevant changes will be reflected by updating the "last updated" date at the beginning of this document.
